Developing Microservices

When developing microservices you need to consider the following capabilities:

  • Elasticity and Resilience
  • Design for Failure
  • Service Discovery
  • Load balancing
  • Pipelines
  • Logging
  • Monitoring
  • Tracing
  • Circuit Breakers
